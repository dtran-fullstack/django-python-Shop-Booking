Project: MyShop

The application has booking system and calculating system for employee
1. Customer can create new appointments:
    a. New appointment will be in pending state.
    a. Can't have more than 2 pending appointments.
    b. Can't have more than 1 pending appointments per one employee.

2. Dashboard:
    a. List of all employees with 2 options view their pay and view their appointments
    b. My pay will allow employee to input their services and automatically calculate paid amount and tips
        i. Show total and tips of 2 weeks
        ii. Show total and tip in one day - This is helpful for daily turn assignment
        iii. Total Tips
        iv. List of services in one day + $
    c. Current appointments and Upcoming Appointment and Pending Appointment(has accept and decline function)
